  • Koala

    Koalas are not actually bears; they are marsupials similar to a wombat.

    • Scientific Name: Phascolarctos cinereus
    • Average Height: 2-3 feet
    • Average Lifespan: 13-18 years
    • Habitat: Eucalyptus Forests in Australia

    Although they have an average lifespan of 13-18 years, koalas are currently endangered due to deforestation and disease and serve as Australia's national symbol for wildlife in that country. wildlife.

  • Little Owl

    Little owls can rotate their necks 270 degrees.

    • Scientific Name: Athene noctua
    • Average Height: 2-3 feet
    • Average Lifespan: 15-16 years
    • Habitat: Eurasia, N. Africa, N. America

    Little owls eat insects, small mammals, birds, and reptiles living primarily near farmlands, woodland fringes, steppes and semi-deserts. These owls are greatly associated with the Greek goddess Athena.
